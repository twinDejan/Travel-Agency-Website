Best Topics

Hotel Booking

Every traveler needs to find somewhere to lay their head, whether it's a place to pitch a tent or a luxury suite in a fancy resort. There's a vast array of accommodation options to choose from, and you have to decide whether to book ahead or look for accommodation after you arrive at the destination.

Preparation

Have you packed everything? Got all the paperwork sorted for your trip? Information on visas, passport and entry requirement for counties. Get ready for your trip.

Health

Health is an issue that affects all travellers, especially to off-the-beaten-path destinations. General common-sense rules apply to all travel. Consult with your doctor beforehand if you have preexisting conditions, verify what insurance coverage you have when away from home, carry a sufficient supply of any medications, and bring copies of your prescriptions in case of loss.

Activities

Many travelers seek out destinations that are particularly suited to specific activities, such as scuba diving or rock climbing. Some of these travel activities require specific gear, training, or planning to pursue, while others may be as simple as slipping into a warm spring to enjoy the soothing waters.

Shopping

Shopping is associated with travel. Travellers might be able to buy items unavailable at home, or items cheaper, or of better quality, than at other places, not to mention souvenirs to remember your trip. Shopping can also be extended to the purchase of travel supplies, before or during travel. Some travellers plan trips around shopping opportunities; for example a trip to Singapore or Hong Kong for clothes or duty-free cameras and electronics.

Transportation

Travel and transportation by land, sea or air go hand in hand, unless the trip is short enough to do by walking. There's lots of options, from crossing oceans and continents by freighter ship or first class in a jet plane, or just getting around town in a taxi or on a bike.
