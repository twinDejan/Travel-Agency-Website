-About Team in Metropol-

We handpick the world's best travel agents and tour companies.

We match you with up to 3 top travel specialists who compete.

These specialists will customize your tour with unique experiences.

"The big idea behind metropol was a simple one. If low-cost airlines can work, so can a low-cost travel agent."

We started metropol.com so we can sell amazing holidays as cheaply as possible. We cut the costs by only selling holidays online (and passing on the savings to you). And instead of expensive advertising, we look after our customers so well that word-of-mouth does the rest. That's why we have thousands of happy customers and can guarantee that any holiday you book with metropol.com will be the lowest possible price.

Launching Metropol.com

We wanted to make something that helped consumers - just like HolidayWatchdog.com - but instead of reviewing holidays, we'd sell them. Cheaply. In fact, we'd sell them so cheaply that when most people visit metropol.com for the first time, they can't quite believe how cheap they are. And that means we've done our job well. We want to sell you and your family the best possible holidays at the lowest possible prices. If we do that, we hope that you'll tell your friends (this helps push our prices even lower). And if we fall short, we want to hear from you - we'll do what we can to put it right.
